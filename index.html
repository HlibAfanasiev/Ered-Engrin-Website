About us

We are a progressive Deathmetalband from Hannover. Since July 2024 we make music together as a Band. Our Style is dark, atmospheric and groovy inspired by chaos, nature and the behaviour of human being. Our sound combines melody, melancholy, brutality, Freedom and creativity to forge a unique identity. We are inspired by Bandprojects like Opeth, Gojira, DreamTheater and many others.
